Ever single people in Japan look forward blooming cherry blossome.

It is different when to bloom cherry blossom every year.

When Spring comes, Japan Meteorological Agency will predict the times of blooming on news.

Normally, it satarts from the south part in March to the North part in May.

The season of cherry blossoms in Tokyo is usually the end of March to the early April.

秋

AUTUMN

Autums is the most comfortable but shortest seasons in Japan. You can enjoy whatever you want to.

Of course, you can enjoy the scenery in Autumn. The streets will be covered by full of coloured leaves such as red and yellow. You will be overwhelmed as much as in Spring.

I'll recommend you to go on picnic to feel Autumn vibes with family or friends.

What's more, Autums is the great season for food. Here are some Autums is the great season for food. Here are some typical Autumn food below.

Rice

Rice is the most important food to Japanese. Autumn is the harvesting season of rice, so that new rice called "新米(shin-mai)" will be ready to line up on our tables. You will be surprised the defferences between normal rice and "新米". It's chewier and sweeter.

Baked sweet potates

Baked sweet potates called "焼き芋(yaki-imo)" is good to eat chilly weather in Autumn.

Chestnuts

Chestnuts are also essencial food in Autumn. We cook them with rice which is called "栗ご飯(kuri-gohan)"

冬

WINTER

I think Winter is the most exiting season because there are many remarkable events in Japan as well as all over the world.

Among them, I'd love to introduce the most important two events for Japases below.

Christmas

Even though we have few Christians in Japan, Christmas is one of the crucial event for us. You may see Christmas lights and decorations anywhere after Halloween which is in November. Many people go out into the tow to enjoy the Christmas atmosphere with family, friends and parters.

New Year

January 1st, it's the start of a brand new year. It's called ”お正月(Oshougatsu)" and has many tradition in Japan. Going to Japan in お正月 may be the best time to get to know Japanese culture.
