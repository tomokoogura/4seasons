FOUR seasons in JAPAN

Japan is one of the beautiful countries in the World.

We have significant scenery, interesting culture, delicious food and so on.

Among them, I am proud that we have FOUR seasons; Spring, Summer, Autumn and Winter.

Each season have unique features. Here is introduction of each season events in JAPAN!

  • SPRING

    The season of meeting and parting in Japan

  • SUMMER

    The season to make remarkable memories

  • AUTUMN

    The season of sports, readind, traveling, eating.... ( up to YOU )

  • WINTER

    The season enjoying full of events

WHEN is it?

Officially March to May

HOW is the weather?

Average High and Low Temperature in Tokyo

  • March 16℃ - 4℃
  • April 20℃ - 10℃
  • May 26℃ - 15℃

WHAT is the seasonal food?

Bamboo shoots, Sakura-mochi, Strawberrys etc

Our Spring will start from March.

The most remarkable event is cherry blossom viewing called お花見.

Somei-yoshino (染井吉野) is planted all over Japan. Normally, it will bloom around March to May from South to North throughout Japan.

SUMMER

Officially June to August

HOW is the weather?

Average High and Low Temperature in Tokyo

Unagi, Somen, Watermelon etc

In the Summer, there are many festival all over Japan. We will wear Yukata (a Japanese traditional cloth) and appreciate fire works.
